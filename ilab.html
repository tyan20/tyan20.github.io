Lamp Project

by Tonny

Nov 28, 2017

This lamp design is a very unique. It was make with woods. It has two wooden stick at the bottom attach to the base then aother wood stick attach to those to at the top. My motivation was from a lamp design online. I think these kind of design is very simple but pretty at the same time.

Skills I learned from this process was researching, designing lamp, wood cutting with machine. It was a very special experice that I have ever had before.
